Under Construction: Automating FCC Reporting Requirements

All television broadcast stations, both commercial and noncommercial, must prepare a list of important issues facing their communities of license and the programs aired during July, August, and September dealing with those issues as part of their broadcast license requirements. This project streamlines the gathering of important stories and topics and reduces the overall workload of report generation. The basic version of this project scrapes the company website and generates a spreadsheet of stories applicable for reporting to the FCC. The upgraded project will integrate with Adobe Analytics to generate a list of the most relevant stories in a quarter. Those stories will be scraped and stored in Firebase. The user frontend will be a webpage that retrieves the top relevant stories from the database. The user will select stories that will be automatically formatted for publication. This is currently under-development.
